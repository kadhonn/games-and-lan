gAmes & lAn in the Grand Garage

In the Halloween Edition of Games & LAN between October 29 and 31, there is something for all game enthusiasts. 60 LAN seats in the Grand Garage offer a great atmosphere for gaming. All those who prefer to play away from the PC, can enjoy the large board game selection, one of the popular Social Deduction rounds (Werewolf, Secret Hitler) or take on the role of a hero in a Pen & Paper role-playing game.

hard facts

  • When? October 29 | 18:00 - October 31 | 12:00
  • Where? Grand Garage, Peter-Behrensplatz 6, 4020 Linz
  • For whom? for all play-enthusiasts, under 16 years an adult accompaniment is necessary

lAn Area

To participate in the LAN a registration with a small fee is necessary. For the 15 Euro registration fee you will get two non-alcoholic drinks and snacks for free during the LAN.

You have to bring your own setup, we provide two sockets and good internet. Please remember to update your game collection at home ;-)

pen & paper

Photo by Jack B on Unsplash

For all those who have always wanted to try a role-playing game, the Games & LAN is the perfect chance to do so! One-shots are the perfect opportunity to take on the role of a hero or heroine in the form of a small role of a hero or heroine in the form of a small adventure. A group of players is guided through a scenario by a game master. The players have to make decisions about how they want to get through the adventure, which can get can be very creative.

On Saturday, there will be several role-playing game rounds taking place in different environments - from classic Dungeon and Dragons to Shadow Run and Blades in the Dark, there will be something for everyone.

If you would like to lead a roleplaying round at the LAN, then write us best of all just send us a mail to pia@linz.coderdojo.net

boardgames Area

In the board game area you will find a large selection of games to borrow. If you wish, you can also bring your own games.

The board games area is opened Friday, October 29 from 19:00 to 02:00 and Saturday, October 30 from 10:00 to 02:00.

Admission is free of charge, children under 16 may only come if accompanied by an adult.

covid-19 info

Due to the current situation of Corona infections and the differences in incidences between the groups of vaccinated and unvaccinated persons, participation in this event is only possible for vaccinated persone. After verification of the vaccination status, no FFP2 mask is to be worn until it has been presented. FFP2 mask obligation.

For all our safety we would like to ask you to do an additional PCR test before the event.

Getting there

By public transport

The Tabakfabrik can be reached by bus with lines 12 or 25. The closest stations are Parkbad and Ludlgasse.

By car

In Ludlgasse near the Tabakfabrik there is a short-term parking zone free of charge. In the Tabakfabrik and in surrounding parking garages there is the possibility of paid parking. Arrival by public transport, on foot or by bicycle is recommended.
